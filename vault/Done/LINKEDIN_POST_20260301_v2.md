I just leveled up my personal productivity stack — and honestly, I didn't expect it to feel this significant.

Over the past few weeks I've been building a **Gold Tier Personal AI Employee** using [Claude Code](https://claude.ai/claude-code) — Anthropic's agentic CLI — and the results have been genuinely impressive.

Here's what the system looks like under the hood:

**The Architecture**
- An Obsidian vault acts as the "brain" — structured folders for Inbox, Needs_Action, Plans, Pending_Approval, Done, and Logs
- Claude Code runs autonomously as the reasoning layer, reading, writing, and routing files
- A human-in-the-loop approval gate sits in front of every sensitive action (email, LinkedIn, payments)
- Every action is logged in JSON for a full audit trail

**What the Gold Tier adds**
- 6 concurrent watchers: email, LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and filesystem
- Automated weekly audit reports with KPI tracking
- CEO-style Monday morning briefings written automatically
- Cloud deployment via Docker + GitHub Actions so it runs 24/7

**The guiding principle:** Local-first, human-in-the-loop, audit everything.

Claude doesn't send emails. Claude doesn't post on social media. Claude doesn't make payments. It *drafts* and *queues* — and I approve. This post itself was drafted by the AI Employee and is sitting in `vault/Pending_Approval/` waiting for my sign-off.

The line between "AI assistant" and "AI employee" is the audit trail and the approval workflow. One you chat with. The other you *manage*.
